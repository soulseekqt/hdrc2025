Fourth Annual HDRC Conference

Rule of Law and International Arbitration

On behalf of Prof. Dr. Gerhard Wagner and the IDR LL.M. Alumni, we are delighted to invite you to the fourth annual Humboldt Dispute Resolution Conference.

This year, the HDRC turns its focus to the Rule of Law in international arbitration—at a time when it faces unprecedented challenges. From the rise of artificial intelligence and climate litigation to fractured supply chains and shifting geopolitical power, the conference explores whether the Rule of Law can hold steady amidst global disruption.

Join us for a day of insightful discussions and networking opportunities. We look forward to welcoming you!
