About the Conference

Where Berlin's spirit of freedom meets the cutting edge of international dispute resolution

The Fourth Annual HDRC

On behalf of Professor Dr. Gerhard Wagner, Academic Director of the International Dispute Resolution (IDR) LL.M., and ten generations of IDR LL.M. Alumni, we are delighted to invite you to the fourth annual Humboldt Dispute Resolution Conference.

This year, the HDRC turns its focus to the Rule of Law in international arbitration—at a time when it faces unprecedented challenges. From the rise of artificial intelligence and climate litigation to fractured supply chains and shifting geopolitical power, the conference explores whether the Rule of Law can hold steady amidst global disruption.
